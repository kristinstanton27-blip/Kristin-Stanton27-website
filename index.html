MEET THE AUTHOR

Kristin Stanton grew up in upstate New York. A trumpet player in her high school marching band, she dreamed of a stable life. But her choices led her down a harrowing path of addiction and life on the streets.

Through these journals, she shares her battle with recovery, her experiences hiking the Appalachian Trail, and her eventual journey toward finding purpose in helping others.
